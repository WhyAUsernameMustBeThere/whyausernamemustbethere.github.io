About this site

Here’s The Salon of Heterodoxy, my personal blog where economics, psychology, other social sciences and philosophy are discussed. The site is built using Github page and Hexo with cactus theme.

The name of this blog comes from a broad tradition of research in the field of economics, namely heterodox economics, which holds different and sometimes radical ideas and theories in contrast to the mainstream economics. The word heterodoxy represents a philosophy similar to the heterodox economics, that is, providing different ideas and thoughts (I hope I can try my best to do that). I will post my reading notes of works done by authors who hold interesting ideas here, specifically, heterodox economics (especially post-Keynesian economics, behavioural economics, and feminist economics), economics of mainstream dissension (mostly analytical Marxism), political and moral philosophy, and psychology.

While personally I am mostly familiar with economics now, the works of psychology and philosophy are still intriguing and these works are likely to be read in the future and related notes or articles will also be posted then. In addition, though I emphasise social sciences and philosophy, you may find articles about other fields (such as natural science) there as well, since I‘ve got a lot of interests. Works of mainstream economics may also be seen here, for the same reason.
